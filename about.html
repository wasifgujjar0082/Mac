Company Overview

At Maxonify, with our primary operations in Miami, Florida, we are a dynamic nearshore software application development company. Our multidisciplinary team of Developers, QA Engineers, Creative Designers, and Project Managers works seamlessly with your team throughout your workday to deliver exceptional results.

We offer diverse engagement models for application development:

  • Full Life-cycle Development
  • Staff Augmentation
  • Dedicated Long Term Engineers

Maxonify specializes in Agile development, leveraging the latest technologies and frameworks in mobile, cloud, web, and business application integration. Our goal is to deliver cost-effective, flexible, and scalable solutions to empower ISVs, startups, and tech-driven companies to achieve a competitive edge. Companies that partner with us consistently return with larger and more critical projects after experiencing our expertise.

We take pride in our experienced, stable team of professionals from diverse backgrounds. Our development teams are highly adaptable and can be resized or realigned to meet customer needs at any time.
